## Key Differentiators

1. **Technology-First Approach**
   - AI-powered vehicle descriptions (Google Gemini)
   - Real-time inventory management (Supabase)
   - Customer relationship management (CRM)

2. **Transparency & Trust**
   - VIN lookup integration (NHTSA API)
   - Detailed vehicle history
   - Blockchain verification (future)

3. **Fractional Ownership** (Future)
   - Access to high-value vehicles via tokenized shares
   - Lower barrier to entry for investors
   - Passive income from vehicle appreciation

4. **NFT Certificates** (Future)
   - Blockchain-verified ownership certificates
   - Immutable vehicle records
   - First dealership in Houston to offer this
