### Channel 4: Referral Program (8% - $4k Year 1)

**Structure:**
```
Customer refers friend →
Friend buys vehicle →
Referrer receives $500 credit or cash

Alternative: Referrer gets $300 cash, buyer gets $200 off
```

**Promotion:**
- Email blast to past customers (monthly)
- Social media posts showcasing referral benefits
- Business cards with referral code

**Tracking:**
- Referral field in lead form
- Track in Supabase (leads.referral_source)
- Auto-calculate rewards in admin dashboard

**Expected Results:**
- 10 referrals in Year 1
- Cost: 10 × $500 = $5,000
- CAC: $500 (higher but ultra-qualified leads)

## Customer Journey & Funnel Optimization

### Awareness Stage

**Channels:** Google Ads, SEO, Social Media

**Goal:** Drive traffic to website

**Content:**
- Blog posts on luxury car buying
- Instagram Reels showcasing inventory
- Google Ads for high-intent keywords

**Metrics:**
- Website visitors: 5,000/month target
- Cost per visitor: $2.00

---

### Consideration Stage

**Page:** Inventory Listing, VIN Lookup, About/Mission

**Goal:** Build trust, demonstrate value

**Tools:**
- Detailed vehicle descriptions (AI-generated)
- VIN lookup tool (transparency)
- Customer testimonials
- "Why Buy from Triple J" page

**Optimization:**
- High-quality photos (25+ per vehicle)
- Video walkarounds
- Comparison tool (vs. competitors)
- Live chat widget (future)

**Metrics:**
- Time on site: 3-5 minutes
- Pages per session: 3+
- Bounce rate: <60%

---

### Decision Stage

**Action:** Submit lead form or call

**Goal:** Capture contact info for follow-up

**Friction Reducers:**
- Simple contact form (name, email, phone, message)
- "No-pressure sales" messaging
- Virtual tour option (Zoom walkthrough)
- Financing calculator (future)

**Metrics:**
- Conversion rate: 7-10% (visitors to leads)
- Form submissions: 350-500/month
- Qualified leads: 35-50/month

---

### Purchase Stage

**Timeline:** 7-14 days average

**Touchpoints:**
1. Initial contact (24 hours)
2. Phone call or in-person showing
3. Negotiation
4. Paperwork & payment
5. Delivery

**Post-Purchase:**
- Thank you email + review request (24 hours)
- Referral program invitation (7 days)
- Follow-up: "How's your [vehicle]?" (30 days)

## Competitive Differentiation Messaging

### vs. Traditional Dealerships

**Their Pitch:** "Trusted name, wide selection, financing options"

**Our Counter:**
- "Lower prices (no expensive showroom overhead)"
- "Full transparency (see our costs, not just the sticker)"
- "Blockchain verification (they don't even know what that is)"

**Messaging:**
*"Why pay dealership markups? We're online-first, so we pass savings to you. Plus, every vehicle comes with a blockchain certificate—something no traditional dealer offers."*

---

### vs. Carvana/Vroom

**Their Pitch:** "Buy online, delivered to your door, 7-day return"

**Our Counter:**
- "Local presence (see cars in person, meet the founders)"
- "Curated luxury selection (not mass-market)"
- "Investment opportunities (fractional ownership)"

**Messaging:**
*"Carvana is great for convenience, but when you're spending $50k+, don't you want to meet the people selling it? We're local, transparent, and offer exclusive investment opportunities."*

---

### vs. Private Sellers

**Their Pitch:** "Best price, no dealer fees"

**Our Counter:**
- "Verified vehicles (NHTSA VIN lookup, inspections)"
- "Reconditioned (no surprises after purchase)"
- "Blockchain certificate (proof of ownership)"
- "Warranty options (peace of mind)"

**Messaging:**
*"Buying from a private seller is a gamble. We verify everything, recondition properly, and give you a blockchain certificate proving authenticity. Worth the small premium for zero risk."*
