<objective>
## Goal
Build the `/inventory/[slug]` vehicle detail page — the single most important conversion page on the site. When a customer clicks a vehicle from the listings or arrives from Facebook Marketplace, this page must present the vehicle compellingly enough to trigger a phone call or lot visit.

## Purpose
Facebook Marketplace traffic lands directly on vehicle detail pages. This is where the sale begins. The page must: (1) show the vehicle beautifully with a gallery, (2) present all specs clearly, (3) make financing feel achievable with a payment calculator, and (4) make it effortless to take action (call, inquire). Every element drives toward conversion.

## Output
- `/inventory/[slug]` dynamic route with server-rendered vehicle detail
- Image gallery component (placeholder-ready, future Supabase Storage images)
- Vehicle specs grid showing all available data
- BHPH payment calculator (simple monthly estimate)
- Prominent call-to-action buttons (Call Now, Inquire)
- Mock data lookup by slug for development without Supabase
- Responsive: mobile-first, premium aesthetic matching Phase 3
</objective>
