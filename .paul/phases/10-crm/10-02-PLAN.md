<objective>
## Goal
Build a CRM pipeline board (kanban-style columns by lead status) and enhance the admin dashboard with CRM-aware stats (leads per stage, sold count, conversion rate).

## Purpose
Jason needs to see his entire sales funnel at a glance — which leads are new, who's been contacted, who has appointments, who's negotiating. The pipeline board replaces scrolling through a flat list with a visual, stage-grouped view. Dashboard stats give instant pulse on sales performance.

## Output
- `/admin/leads/board` — Kanban-style pipeline board with leads grouped by status columns
- Enhanced admin dashboard with lead pipeline breakdown stats
- Board link in admin sidebar navigation
</objective>
