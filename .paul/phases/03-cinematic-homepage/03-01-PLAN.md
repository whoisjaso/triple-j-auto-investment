<objective>
## Goal
Build the cinematic scroll-driven homepage with three Apple-style frame animations (Maybach, Keys, Crest) on a dark luxury aesthetic (Black + Dark Forest Green + Gold), with Lenis buttery-smooth scrolling.

## Purpose
The homepage IS the brand experience. Every scroll pixel engineers the perception of opulence — Loro Piana, Rolls Royce, Apple.com-level craftsmanship. This is not a dealership template; it is a cinematic product reveal that positions Triple J as aspirational luxury in the $3-8K market.

## Output
- Three scroll-driven canvas animation sections stacked vertically
- Lenis smooth scroll wrapper for "floating" scroll feel
- Loading state per section (batched frame preloading)
- Dark luxury color scheme throughout
- Text overlays with gold serif typography tied to scroll position
- Fully working homepage at localhost:3000
</objective>
